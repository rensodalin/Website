Our Story

Our journey from bean to cup started in year 2000. Cafe culture had not set its footprint and coffee was still considered a luxury. Our quest to provide a true coffee experience and brew the perfect cup led us to create the brand Barista. Spearheading the Indian cafe culture, Barista is an opportunity to discover the craft of coffee.

2000

Foundation

Barista was established in the year 2000 with the mission to provide coffee lovers with an authentic and memorable coffee experience. Born out of a passion for high-quality coffee, Barista set out to create a space where people could enjoy expertly brewed beverages made from the finest beans.

2005

Expansion

We opened our first flagship store with the goal of bringing the rich and vibrant Indian cafe culture to a wider audience. This marked a significant milestone in our journey, as we aimed to create a unique space where people could experience the warmth and richness of Indian coffee traditions.

2010

Innovations

We introduced a variety of new brews, including refreshing iced coffees and our own signature blends, which quickly gained popularity among coffee enthusiasts.
